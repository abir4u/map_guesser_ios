Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Map Guessr Challenge collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect data that is necessary for the core functionality of the app:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google OAuth, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to create your user profile.
  • Game Progress: We store your high scores, game history, and statistics in our secure database (MongoDB) so you can sync them across devices.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic diagnostic data (e.g., OS version) to improve app performance and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Providing and maintaining your game account.
  • Displaying your progress and ranking on leaderboards.
  • Improving the user experience and app functionality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with service providers (like MongoDB for data storage and Google for authentication) to the extent necessary to run the app.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To request deletion, please contact us at the email address below.

5. Children's Privacy

Map Guessr Challenge does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.
