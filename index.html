Map Guessr Challenge

5 Attempts. 1 Mystery Country. How well do you know the world?

About the App

Map Guessr is a fast-paced geographic discovery game for iOS. Players are presented with a map outline and must identify the country. Not sure where you are? Every guess provides a directional hint and distance clue, guiding you toward the correct location across the globe.

Game Features

  • Single Player Mode LIVE: Perfect your skills and beat your personal best.
  • Play with Friends IN DEVELOPMENT: Challenge your friends to see who is the ultimate navigator.
  • Global Leaderboard COMING SOON: Compete with players worldwide.
  • Secure Sync: Sign in with Google OAuth to keep your stats synced across devices.

The Tech Stack

Built for performance and scalability:

  • Python Backend
  • MongoDB
